Crystals Collector!

How to Play:


You will be given a random number at the start of each game.

Click on each crystal and you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

In order to win, you need to match your total score to the random number given, but be careful! You will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from yo until you click on it.

After each game, the values of each crystal will change.
